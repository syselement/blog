| ------------------------------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ | -------------------------------- |

| [jayofelony/pwnagotchi](https://github.com/jayofelony/pwnagotchi) | Modern maintained Pwnagotchi fork            | AI-style Wi-Fi companion, Pi Zero 2 W builds           | Most active Pwnagotchi fork      |
